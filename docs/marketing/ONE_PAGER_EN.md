## Design Specifications

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Deep Blue (#1a365d) - Professional, trustworthy
- **Accent**: Bright Blue (#3182ce) - Technology
- **Secondary**: Green (#38a169) - Success, growth
- **Background**: White/Light Gray (#f7fafc)

### Typography
- **Headline**: Inter Bold 24pt
- **Subheadline**: Inter Medium 18pt
- **Body**: Inter Regular 14pt
- **Data Highlights**: Inter Bold 36pt

### Visual Layout
- Left 1/3: Logo + Product screenshots
- Middle 1/3: Core value proposition + Feature list
- Right 1/3: ROI calculation + Contact info

### Print Specifications
- Size: US Letter (8.5" x 11") or A4 (210mm x 297mm)
- Paper: 100lb Gloss Cover
- Finish: Double-sided lamination
